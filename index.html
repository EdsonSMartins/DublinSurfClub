Dublin Surf Club Core Advantages

Best Surf Experience

Fitted with the latest features for adaptive surfers while offering great waves for surfers of all experience levels.




Limited Crowd

Surfer numbers are capped per session and surfer priority is managed with a strict but fair one-at-a-time system.

Safety first

Surf Etiquette provided in order to ensure a safe and controlled environment for surfing.




Surf Academy

Great variety of surfing programs where you will be training with the same coach, at the same time each week. Your progress will be accelerated through tailored instruction and weekly repetition.

Healthy body and mind

Proximity to water is associated with many positive measures of physical and mental wellbeing, from higher levels of vitamin D to better social relations.




Connecting Talents

Very unique mentoring experience with professionals surfers
